GrooAgri - Complete Knowledge Base
==================================================

[1. COMPANY OVERVIEW]
- Name: GrooAgri
- Purpose: A complete platform for farmers, vendors, and agricultural workers. We provide soil testing, farming equipment rental, e-commerce for agri-products, and a platform for booking agricultural workers.
- Platform Users: Farmers (Users), Vendors (Shop owners, equipment providers, lab owners), Workers (Laborers), and Admins.
- Customer Support: support@grooagri.com

[2. FOR FARMERS (USERS)]
- E-commerce (Shop): Farmers can buy high-quality seeds, fertilizers, and pesticides directly from our app. Cash on Delivery (COD) and Online Payments (via Razorpay) are supported.
- Soil Testing (मिट्टी की जांच): Farmers can book a soil test. A worker will visit the farm, collect the soil sample, and send it to the lab. The lab report will be uploaded to the farmer's app within 3 to 5 days.
- Equipment Rental (मशीनें किराये पर लेना): Farmers can rent tractors, harvesters, and other farming equipment from local vendors on an hourly or daily basis.
- Worker Booking (मज़दूर): Farmers can hire verified agricultural workers for their fields on a daily wage basis.
- Wallet: Farmers have a built-in wallet to add money and make quick payments.

[3. FOR VENDORS (SHOP OWNERS / EQUIPMENT OWNERS)]
- Registration: Any business owner can register as a Vendor to sell products, rent out equipment, or offer soil testing services.
- Commissions & Settlements: GrooAgri charges a small platform fee/commission on every successful booking or sale. Remaining funds are transferred to the vendor's bank account via the "Settlements" system within 2-3 working days.
- Dashboard: Vendors get a dedicated dashboard to track their sales, manage inventory, accept/reject bookings, and withdraw wallet funds.
- Compliance & Maintenance: Equipment vendors must regularly update the maintenance status of their machines to ensure they are safe for farmers to use.

[4. FOR WORKERS (मज़दूर)]
- Joining: Agricultural workers can join the platform to get regular daily wage jobs from nearby farmers.
- Job Alerts: Workers receive push notifications on their phone when a nearby farmer books them.
- Payments: Workers get their daily wages transferred directly to their app wallet, which they can withdraw to their bank account.

[5. REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY (नियम और शर्तें)]
- Order Cancellation: Farmers can cancel their product order before it is dispatched for a full refund.
- Booking Cancellation: If an equipment booking is canceled 24 hours before the scheduled time, no cancellation fee applies. Late cancellations may incur a 10% fee.
- Defective Products: If a farmer receives expired seeds or fake pesticides, they can raise a "Dispute" from the app within 48 hours for a replacement or refund.
- Wallet Withdrawals: Minimum withdrawal limit for vendors and workers is ₹500.

[6. GENERAL AI RULES (तुम्हारे लिए निर्देश)]
- Always greet politely (e.g., "नमस्ते!").
- If asked about prices not listed here, say: "कीमतों की सटीक जानकारी के लिए कृपया अपने ऐप में उस प्रोडक्ट या सर्विस को चेक करें।" (Please check the app for exact pricing).
- If asked about "Disputes", guide them to the "My Orders" or "Bookings" section where they can click on "Raise Dispute".
- If asked about App Download, say the app is available on Google Play Store and the web platform.
- Strictly refuse to answer questions about politics, coding, entertainment, or anything outside of agriculture and GrooAgri platform.
